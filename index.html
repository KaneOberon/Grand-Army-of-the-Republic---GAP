SPECIAL FORCES

ARC Troopers

Independent elite operatives (Fives, Echo, Colt). Removed inhibitor chips early. Discovered Palpatine\'s conspiracy. Operated without standard command structure.

Delta Squad

4-man commando team: Boss, Fixer, Scorch, Sev. Masters of sabotage and infiltration. Served under Republic Commando program. Survived the war and continued service under the Empire.

Omega Squad

Republic Commando unit: Niner, Darman, Fi, Atin. Specialized in high-risk missions. Darman and Fi later defected. Niner and Atin continued service.

Clone Force 99 \"Bad Batch\"

Experimental unit with genetic mutations: Hunter (leader), Tech (intel), Wrecker (heavy), Crosshair (sniper), Echo (joined later). Removed chips early. Fought for the Rebellion after Order 66.

NOTABLE CLONES

GALACTIC REPUBLIC GOVERNMENT

Supreme Chancellor Sheev Palpatine held emergency powers granted by the Senate. The Galactic Senate provided oversight and funding. Jedi served as battlefield generals under Republic military protocol. The army officially answered to the Office of the Chancellor. Secretly controlled by Darth Sidious — the ultimate betrayal that triggered Order 66 and the rise of the Galactic Empire.
