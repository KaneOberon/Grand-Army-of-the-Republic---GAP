JEDI GENERALS & HIGH COUNCIL

Obi-Wan Kenobi

High Jedi General of the 212th Attack Battalion. Master of Soresu. Known as "The Negotiator". Led campaigns on Christophsis, Utapau, and Teth. Survived Order 66, exiled on Tatooine to protect Luke Skywalker. Died confronting Darth Vader on the Death Star in 0 BBY.

Anakin Skywalker

Jedi General of the 501st Legion. Nicknamed "The Hero With No Fear". Master pilot and duelist. Commanded Torrent Company with Ahsoka Tano. Turned to the dark side as Darth Vader. Survived Order 66 and became the Emperor's enforcer until redeemed in 4 ABY.

Plo Koon

High Jedi General of the 104th "Wolfpack" Battalion. Kel Dor ace pilot and master of the Force. Led multiple rescue missions. Known for his wisdom and loyalty to clones. Killed in action during Order 66 when his starfighter was shot down by his own troops.

Mace Windu

Jedi Master and senior member of the High Council. Creator of Vaapad form. Led the 91st Reconnaissance Corps. Attempted to arrest Chancellor Palpatine. Defeated in duel and presumed killed during Order 66, though some legends suggest survival.

Aayla Secura

Jedi General of the 327th Star Corps. Twi'lek master of Form IV Ataru. Fought on Felucia and many Outer Rim campaigns. Known for her grace and skill with dual sabers. Executed by her troops during Order 66 on Felucia.

Shaak Ti

Jedi Master and High Council member. Oversaw clone training on Kamino. Protected the Jedi Temple during the war. Survived initial Order 66 but was later killed on Felucia (or Coruscant in some accounts) by Darth Vader's forces.

LAAT/i Republic Gunship

Primary troop transport and gunship. Equipped with mass-driver cannons, laser turrets, and missile launchers. Carried up to 30 clones. Used in every major landing operation. Iconic "larty" of the Clone Wars. Many variants existed including the LAAT/c cargo version.
